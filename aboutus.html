About Us

LevelOne Fashion began trading in March 2002 in a small store in County Kerry, Ireland. Over the years our company has grown and expanded throughout Ireland and in 2007 we expanded across the sea into the UK. Now we have a total of 64 stores spread across the United Kingdom and Ireland with plans in place to begin expansion to mainland Europe in the coming year.

All our clothing is manufactured locally with materials sourced as close to home as possible, creating hundreds of jobs around Ireland. We believe staying true to our heritage is more important than the profits to be gained from outsourcing to cheap labourers abroad.

When you buy from PlayerOne Fashion you know you're getting only the highest textile quality available across our entire range of clothing.
